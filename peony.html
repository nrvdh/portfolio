CONCEPT

During this minor, I focussed on several trends and problems. My main topics we’re screentime and self-improvement. Nowadays we are living a busy life. We have a lot on our minds such as social activities, work-related stuff, mental health, expectations, and our own thoughts. All these things are connected to the use of screentime. For work, we use our laptops for hours, when we want to connect we grab our phones and when we are seeking for some people we put on the television. We are constantly removing ourselves from the real world. We don’t look around, our comfort zone is getting smaller and we are losing balance. Too much screentime can cause negative feelings like feeling useless and out of focus. My main goal was to break our border with the real world to create a more balanced life

CONCEPT

"How might we balance our lives by living offline?"

During my minor I created a smart plant called PEONY.
PEONY consists out of artificial peonies. They look like real flowers but they aren’t. The plant will grow based on data. This data can get collected by completing PEONY missions.

When you buy PEONY you will receive the plant and a card box with missions. These missions will help you balancing your life. The goal is to finish one mission a day. The missions are related to topics like social communication, mindfullness and stepping out of your comfort zone. These missions will lead you away from your phone and show you more of the real world around you. You can choose a big mission or a small mission; based on your available time. You've finished a mission when you think it was valuable for you. When you think it’s finished you can validate the mission in the PEONY pot. You can put the card in the slit. The pot will scan the card and when its done the peonies will grow. The plant reflects your own growth. After a while, the plant will be full grown. This means you will have a bush full of peonies.

A bush full of peonies means a lucky and happy life. You can keep the plant like this, as a symbol/achievement for yourself. Aslo, you can give the plant to someone you want to experience a happy and lucky life. We are used to giving flowers or plants as a gift but with PEONY we can make this even more valuable.
