ABOUT

Hi there!
My name is Naomi van den Heuvel. I am a 21 year old communication and multimedia design student. At the moment I'm finishing my studies and I'm looking for a suitable internship.

Lately I've been doing research on cultural differences and programming my own portfolio website (which you're looking at now!). I am interested in different cultures, norms and values. I find it special to see that each person has a different view on what is 'normal'. When designing products or services, I try to focus on the value of the target group in question.

My passion lies in coming up with new and diverse concepts and visualising them. The hobbies I like to work with are illustration, analogue photography and lifestyle.

I love innovation, calmness, the change of seasons and discovering new places.
