ABOUT

The digiscan is an online questionnaire made by 'Kamer van Koophandel' in collaboration with 'Kenniscentrum Business Innovation'. Informing, inspiring and advising SME entrepreneurs about digitization is the goal of this Digiscan.

"How do you turn a boring questionnaire into an inspiring and motivating online experience for small and medium-sized entrepreneurs who have insufficient time to think about the development of their business?"

During this project I worked togheter with three other students. My functions during this project were concepting, research and visual design. We went through five stages: research, concept, create, testing and finalizing.

-

CONCEPT

DIGILEMMA
This concept focuses on stimulating digitization. By moving into a dilemma, the entrepreneur starts to think about realistic situations that have to do with digital developments within their field. By having entrepreneurs move into a story, they think much more consciously about choices they make.

To prevent a "drop out" moment during the game, we ensure that there is constant interaction between input and output . When you, as an entrepreneur, have solved the dilemma, you will immediately see what the possible consequences for a company may be in the short and long term. In this way the entrepreneur is immediately made to think about the consequences of his or her personal company.

After all dilemmas have been finished, a clear results page will appear where you will see a retrospective of all your answers. Otto also gives you personal advice on how digitized you are and in which areas you can challenge yourself even more.
